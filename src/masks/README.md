### Masks

ABCD_group_7net_subcortex.dscalar.nii: subcortical 7-network parcellation in the ABCD dataset.

HCP_group_7net_subcortex.dscalar.nii: subcortical 7-network parcellation in the HCP dataset.

fslr_downsample_900mesh_parcellation.dlabel.nii: 1483 ROIs on the cortical sheet. Derived from Kong et al., 2019, Cerebral Cortex.

Atlas_ROIs.2.nii.gz: subcortical mask defined in both HCP grayordinate space and FreeSurfer space.
